Frequently asked questions

How do I delete my account?

Email us at cansteydev@gmail.com from the address you used to register, with the subject "Delete my account." We will permanently delete your account and all associated data within 30 days.

Where do the prices come from?

Estimated values are computed from active eBay marketplace listings, refreshed daily. Prices reflect what comparable figures are currently being asked, not necessarily what they sell for. Prices are estimates and should not be relied on for insurance or financial decisions.

Is my collection visible to other users?

No. Your collection is private and visible only to you. We use database-level row security to enforce this — even our own engineers cannot accidentally see your data through the app.

Can I use the app without internet?

The app requires an internet connection to load your collection and figure data. Offline mode is on the roadmap for a future update.

How do I export my collection?

Export to CSV is on the roadmap. In the meantime, email us and we'll provide your data in a machine-readable format.

I found a figure that's missing from the catalog. Can you add it?

Yes — email us the figure name, line, year (if known), and a reference link. We add new figures regularly.
